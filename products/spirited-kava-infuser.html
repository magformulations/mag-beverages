How to serve

Great straight over ice — but the Infuser is built to mix. A 2 oz pour stands in for a shot in any build. Three to start:

No. 1

Mango Drift

Tropical, easy, crowd-friendly

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Spirited Kava Infuser
  • 3 oz fresh mango juice
  • 1 oz sparkling water
  • Squeeze of lime
  • Ice
Method

Combine the Infuser, mango juice and lime over ice; top with sparkling water and stir once.

No. 2

The Pacific Tonic

Earthy, bitter, clean

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Spirited Kava Infuser
  • 4 oz premium tonic water
  • 2 fresh mint sprigs
  • Squeeze of lemon
  • Ice
Method

Clap the mint and add to a tall glass of ice. Pour the Infuser, lemon, then tonic. Don’t over-stir.

No. 3

Dark Cherry Ritual

Low-light drink energy

Ingredients
  • 2 oz Spirited Kava Infuser
  • 2 oz pomegranate juice
  • 1 oz tart cherry juice
  • 2 oz sparkling water
  • Ice
Method

Build the Infuser, pomegranate and cherry over ice; top with sparkling water and garnish with rosemary.
